# Scheme Circular: Growth Booster - OPC RJ FY25

**Scheme ID:** SCHBLD011
**Issued by:** DuraBuild Trade Marketing
**Status:** expired
**Effective period:** 2024-10-01 to 2025-03-31

## 1. Eligibility
This scheme is open to channel partners of tier A/B/C operating in Rajasthan. Partners must have an active trading account and no unresolved claim disputes.

## 2. Scope of products
Applicable SKUs: BLD-OPC53, BLD-PPC.

## 3. Incentive structure (QPS mode, running slab, cash)
- Growth over baseline >= 5%  ->  payout 1.0% of eligible turnover
- Growth over baseline >= 10%  ->  payout 1.75% of eligible turnover
- Growth over baseline >= 18%  ->  payout 2.5% of eligible turnover

## 4. Baseline & measurement
Uplift is measured against the trailing_12m baseline for the eligible SKUs and region. Only secondary-verified sell-out counts towards slab achievement.

## 5. Claim process
Claims must be raised within 30 days of period close with supporting sell-out evidence. Payout is by cash. Over-claims beyond earned entitlement will be recovered from the next settlement.

## 6. Budget & caps
Total scheme budget: Rs 4,200,000. The company may cap total per-invoice discount where multiple schemes apply.
