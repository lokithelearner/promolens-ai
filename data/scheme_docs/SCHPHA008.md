**Scheme ID:** SCHPHA008

**Effective period:** 2023-10-01 to 2024-03-31

## 1. Eligibility
This scheme is open to channel partners of tier A/B/C operating in Mumbai. Partners must have an active trading account and no unresolved claim disputes.

## 2. Scope of products
Applicable SKUs: PHA-CARD10, PHA-CARD5, PHA-TELS80.

## 4. Baseline & measurement
Uplift is measured against the trailing_12m baseline for the eligible SKUs and region. Only secondary-verified sell-out counts towards slab achievement.

## 5. Claim process
Claims must be raised within 30 days of period close with supporting sell-out evidence. Payout is by cash. Over-claims beyond earned entitlement will be recovered from the next settlement.

## 6. Budget & caps
Total scheme budget: Rs 2,200,000. The company may cap total per-invoice discount where multiple schemes apply.
