# Scheme Circular: Metabolic QPS - South FY26

**Scheme ID:** SCHPHA029
**Issued by:** NovaCure Trade Marketing
**Status:** expired
**Effective period:** 2025-10-01 to 2026-03-31

## 1. Eligibility
This scheme is open to channel partners of tier A/B/C operating in Vijayawada. Partners must have an active trading account and no unresolved claim disputes.

## 2. Scope of products
Applicable SKUs: PHA-ATOR10, PHA-GLIM2.

## 3. Incentive structure (QPS mode, linear slab, cash)
- {"target_value": 500000, "payout_pct": 2.5}

## 4. Baseline & measurement
Uplift is measured against the trailing_12m baseline for the eligible SKUs and region. Only secondary-verified sell-out counts towards slab achievement.

## 5. Claim process
Claims must be raised within 30 days of period close with supporting sell-out evidence. Payout is by cash. Over-claims beyond earned entitlement will be recovered from the next settlement.

## 6. Budget & caps
Total scheme budget: Rs 1,600,000. The company may cap total per-invoice discount where multiple schemes apply.
