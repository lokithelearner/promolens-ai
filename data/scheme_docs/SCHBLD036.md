# Scheme Circular: Q2 Draft Proposal - PPC

**Scheme ID:** SCHBLD036
**Issued by:** DuraBuild Trade Marketing
**Status:** draft
**Effective period:** 2026-07-01 to 2026-09-30

## 1. Eligibility
This scheme is open to channel partners of tier A/B/C operating in Gujarat. Partners must have an active trading account and no unresolved claim disputes.

## 2. Scope of products
Applicable SKUs: BLD-PPC.

## 3. Incentive structure (QPS mode, running slab, cash)
- Growth over baseline >= 6%  ->  payout 1.25% of eligible turnover

## 4. Baseline & measurement
Uplift is measured against the trailing_12m baseline for the eligible SKUs and region. Only secondary-verified sell-out counts towards slab achievement.

## 5. Claim process
Claims must be raised within 30 days of period close with supporting sell-out evidence. Payout is by cash. Over-claims beyond earned entitlement will be recovered from the next settlement.

## 6. Budget & caps
Total scheme budget: Rs 2,500,000. The company may cap total per-invoice discount where multiple schemes apply.
