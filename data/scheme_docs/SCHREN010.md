# Scheme Circular: Elite Club FY24

**Scheme ID:** SCHREN010
**Issued by:** SunPeak Trade Marketing
**Status:** expired
**Effective period:** 2023-10-01 to 2024-03-31

## 1. Eligibility
This scheme is open to channel partners of tier Gold/Silver operating in ALL. Partners must have an active trading account and no unresolved claim disputes.

## 2. Scope of products
Applicable SKUs: REN-PNL540, REN-PNL450, REN-INV5K, REN-INV10K.

## 3. Incentive structure (QPS mode, fixed slab, credit_note)
- Growth over baseline >= 10%  ->  payout 1.0% of eligible turnover
- Growth over baseline >= 20%  ->  payout 2.0% of eligible turnover

## 4. Baseline & measurement
Uplift is measured against the trailing_12m baseline for the eligible SKUs and region. Only secondary-verified sell-out counts towards slab achievement.

## 5. Claim process
Claims must be raised within 30 days of period close with supporting sell-out evidence. Payout is by credit_note. Over-claims beyond earned entitlement will be recovered from the next settlement.

## 6. Budget & caps
Total scheme budget: Rs 5,000,000. The company may cap total per-invoice discount where multiple schemes apply.
