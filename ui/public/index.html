How to read this brief. Every figure is computed by a deterministic Python engine over the manufacturer's promotion tables in BigQuery — never generated by the language model; Gemini narrates and routes. ROI = incremental value net of scheme outflow, measured against a trailing-12-month baseline for the scheme's SKUs and region. Growth on the table sums the Next-Best-Scheme upside across eligible partners (each on its own base × the scheme's slab × that partner's historical uptake). Untapped upside benchmarks each partner against its same-tier peer median. Leaking now = claims above earned entitlement + inventory-loading signals.
